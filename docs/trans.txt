5. Author's fee: We have two types of fee apply for user who want to sell their products on our system.
 - For current authors (no account registration): fixed fee is 35% of total revenue of each product.
 - For regular authors (registered account): the starting fee is 25% of total revenue of each product.
   This fee will be descrease base on some criteria such as the total sales achieved, the score from the purchase.
   And not less than 10%
